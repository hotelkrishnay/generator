A Legacy of Excellence

Capital Group Crystal Sea Food Group

Capital Group, operating under the flagship brand Crystal Sea Food Group, is a diversified seafood enterprise with over a decade of experience in the seafood industry.

The journey of Crystal Sea Food Group began more than ten years ago as a reliable supplier of fresh fish to leading frozen seafood exporters. With deep industry knowledge, strong sourcing networks, and a clear understanding of global market demand, the Group identified opportunities in value-added processing and international exports.

Over time, the Group established its own seafood processing and export operations, delivering high-quality frozen seafood products to international markets. Further strengthening its portfolio, the Group also ventured into the processing and export of dry fish maw, catering to specialized global demand.

Despite significant growth and diversification, the Group has remained firmly connected to its roots. The fresh fish supply business continues to be a core strength, providing direct access to abundant raw material sources. This integrated supply chain enables the Group to procure seafood at economical rates, maintain strict quality control, and consistently meet buyer requirements with superior quality products at competitive prices.

Year after year, Crystal Sea Food Group has emerged as a trusted and dynamic name in the seafood industry, driven by integrity, consistency, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be a globally respected seafood group delivering high-quality, responsibly sourced products while creating long-term value for customers, partners, and stakeholders.

  • Sourcing Expertise: Leveraging our supply chain to offer the most competitive global pricing.

  • Sustainability: Growing while staying rooted in our core values.
